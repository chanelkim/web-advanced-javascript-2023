Court Finder

Tennis Courts Near You

Finding a public tennis court in New York City shouldn't be difficult. While there are multiple online resources dedicated to listing where tennis courts can be found, I haven't come across a single site that provides a list according to your location. This tool seeks to help you find NYC tennis courts easily. Enter any location within the five NYC boroughs and the search will offer places to play based on the zipcode associated with your input.
