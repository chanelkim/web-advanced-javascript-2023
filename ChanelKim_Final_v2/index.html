Finding a public tennis court in New York City shouldn't be difficult. Multiple resources online may list courts found throughout the city but there's not a single source that locates them according to where you are now.

Court Finder was developed to make this possible. Enter any location within the five boroughs and our search tool will serve you a list of nearby courts. Alternate places for play are also provided, in case the closest court is in a different zipcode.
